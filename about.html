About PermianAgentic

PermianAgentic is an Autonomous Basin Intelligence Platform developed for oil and gas operations, SWD injection management, induced seismicity forecasting, and seismic risk reduction in the Permian Basin.

The platform integrates production analytics, SWD injection monitoring, GIS-based basin intelligence, Transformer time-series forecasting, spatial b-value risk mapping, Reinforcement Learning optimization, and Agentic AI decision systems within a unified environment.

PermianAgentic is designed to transform traditional reactive subsurface monitoring into proactive and autonomous decision support for safer and more efficient energy operations.

Platform Capabilities

  • Oil, gas, and water production analytics
  • SWD injection monitoring and optimization
  • Induced seismicity mapping and forecasting
  • Seismicity-time and seismicity-depth analysis
  • GIS-based basin intelligence
  • Spatial b-value risk assessment
  • Transformer-based predictive modeling
  • Reinforcement Learning-based injection control
  • Agentic AI autonomous decision framework
  • Industry-focused technical reporting
