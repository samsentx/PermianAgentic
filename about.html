About PermianAgentic

PermianAgentic is an Agentic AI platform focused on SWD injection control, b-value based induced seismic risk forecasting, and seismic risk mitigation across the Permian Basin.

The platform integrates b-value analytics, transformer-based forecasting, reinforcement learning, and geomechanical intelligence to support proactive operational decision-making in energy operations.

PermianAgentic was founded in 2026 in Houston, Texas with the goal of advancing AI-driven solutions for safer and more efficient subsurface operations.

Platform Capabilities

  • b-value based induced seismic risk forecasting
  • SWD injection optimization and control
  • Seismic risk mitigation strategies
  • Agentic AI decision-support systems
  • Geomechanical intelligence and monitoring
  • Operational risk assessment

Technology Stack

  • Python – Data processing and AI development
  • Transformer Models – Time-series forecasting
  • Reinforcement Learning – Adaptive control strategies
  • TensorFlow / PyTorch – Deep learning frameworks
  • Pandas / NumPy – Scientific computing
  • Geospatial Analytics – Spatial risk assessment
  • Geomechanics Analytics – Subsurface risk interpretation
  • HTML / CSS / JavaScript – Web platform interface
  • GitHub Cloud Infrastructure – Deployment and version control
