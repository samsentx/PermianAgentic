About PermianAgentic

PermianAgentic is an advanced Agentic AI platform developed to support safer and more efficient Saltwater Disposal (SWD) operations across the Permian Basin. The platform combines induced seismicity forecasting, b-value analytics, reinforcement learning, and transformer-based artificial intelligence to provide proactive operational intelligence for energy operators.

Our mission is to help reduce induced seismicity risk while maintaining operational efficiency through intelligent, data-driven decision support. By continuously analyzing seismic activity patterns, injection operations, and basin-scale geospatial information, PermianAgentic delivers actionable recommendations for risk-aware injection management.

What We Do

  • Monitor and analyze induced seismicity trends.
  • Identify elevated seismic risk zones using b-value analytics.
  • Forecast future seismic risk and potential magnitude escalation.
  • Provide adaptive SWD injection recommendations.
  • Support proactive seismic risk mitigation strategies.
  • Deliver basin-scale operational intelligence through Agentic AI.

Technology

PermianAgentic integrates multiple advanced technologies including Transformer Models, Reinforcement Learning, Geospatial Analytics, Geomechanical Intelligence, and Agentic AI frameworks. These technologies work together to transform complex subsurface and operational data into meaningful recommendations that support informed decision-making.

Vision

Our vision is to create the next generation of intelligent energy management systems capable of autonomously evaluating risk, optimizing operational decisions, and improving environmental stewardship across energy-producing regions worldwide.

Permian Basin Focus

The platform is specifically designed for the Permian Basin, one of the world's most active oil and gas producing regions. By focusing on basin-scale data integration and continuous learning, PermianAgentic aims to provide a practical and scalable solution for induced seismicity risk management.
